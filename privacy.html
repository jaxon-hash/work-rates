PRIVACY NOTICE.

A clear summary of what the project form collects, why it is needed and how to request its removal.

LAST UPDATED / 22 JULY 2026

Questions about your information can be sent to business.jxnn@gmail.com.

01

Information collected

The project form collects the name, email address, project requirements, budget range, deadline, optional Discord username and links that you choose to provide.

02

How it is used

This information is used to review your enquiry, prepare a quote, reply to you and—if you decide to proceed—help organise the project.

03

Storage and access

Enquiries are protected by Cloudflare Turnstile, checked securely on the server and stored using Supabase. They can only be viewed through the studio’s authenticated private dashboard. They are not displayed publicly or sold to third parties.

04

Retention and removal

Enquiry details are kept only while reasonably needed to discuss, manage or document the potential project. You can request access, correction or deletion by emailing the studio.

05

Links and sensitive information

Please do not submit passwords, payment-card details or other sensitive personal information. Any external footage or reference link remains subject to the access settings chosen on its original service.
