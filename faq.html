01 / PRODUCTION INTEL

The useful stuff, without the waffle.

01How do revisions work?+
Every project includes structured review rounds. Minor pacing changes, cut adaptations and asset replacements are handled quickly to keep the production timeline clean.
02What is your typical turnaround time?+
Turnaround depends on your channel scale and the complexity of the edit. Short-form edits typically wrap within 24–72 hours, while more involved YouTube videos usually take 2–5 days. Faster delivery can be arranged when discussed early.
03How should I send over project files?+
Bundle raw footage, screen captures, voice tracks, logos and custom overlays in organised folders. Google Drive, Dropbox or Frame.io links all work well.
04Do you offer long-term monthly retainers?+
Yes. Creators who need consistent output can reserve a monthly editing schedule, giving them priority production slots and more predictable turnaround times.
