Frequently Asked Questions

What is your average turnaround time?
Standard edits typically take 2-5 business days depending on complexity, this varies depending on how big your channel is. Shorts and TikToks are usually delivered within 24-48 hours.
What software do you use for editing?
I primarily use Adobe Premiere Pro for the main assembly and After Effects for advanced motion graphics and visual effects.
How many revisions do I get?
I offer 2 rounds of minor revisions for free to ensure the final product matches your vision perfectly.
How should I send my footage?
You can send files via Google Drive, WeTransfer, or Dropbox. Please ensure the link is set to "Anyone with the link can view/download."
How do payments work?
I usually require a 50% deposit upfront for new clients, with the remaining balance due upon final approval of the edit.
